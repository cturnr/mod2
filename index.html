Chicken & Waffles

Whisk the hot sauce and egg in a medium bowl; toss the chicken in the mixture to coat. Combine the flour, poultry seasoning, and salt and pepper to taste in a medium bowl.

Candied Bacon

Preheat oven to 325F. Put bacon in a bowl, season w/ pepper and toss with brown sugar. Cover a baking sheet with parchment or foil and arrange the bacon in a single layer. Bake for 20 minutes.

Very Bloody Mary

In 11-ounce highball glass, stir together tomato juice, vodka, Worcestershire sauce, horseradish, hot sauce, salt, and pepper. Fill glass with ice, then pour mixture into second glass.
